About BMI

BMI is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height. Here are the categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

Maintaining a healthy BMI is important for your overall health. Use our calculator to determine your BMI and get personalized health recommendations.
